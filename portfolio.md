Specifically what you're describing is called:
"Sticky Scroll" or "Scroll Reveal"

Exactly what it does:

Page doesn't move or scroll normally
Content slides, fades, or animates INTO the screen as you scroll
Feels like content is being revealed, not navigated to
Screen stays fixed, elements come to it


The specific effect you want is called:
"Framer Motion Scroll Reveal" or "GSAP ScrollTrigger"




"In the about section, use Framer Motion scroll-triggered animations. When the user scrolls to the about section, the text content should fade in and slide up from below. The cards should animate in one by one with a staggered delay — each card appearing 0.1s after the previous one. The section should feel like content is being revealed as you scroll, not like a page jump."

Libraries to use:

Framer Motion — easiest, you're already using Next.js so it installs directly
GSAP ScrollTrigger — more powerful, slightly more complex
